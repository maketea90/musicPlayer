### A music player with copyright free music built with React-Native.

- Install dependencies: *npm install*

- To run, execute: *npx expo start*

- Tap a song in the list to play it. Filter songs by whether your search term is in the artist name or the track title, using the search bar and button.

- Click the X in the search bar to remove filtering.

- When a song is clicked an overlay appears showing the track, album artwork and artist, and a progress bar with a play/pause button.

- Click the overlay to get a more detailed look at the current track (nowPlaying screen).

- From the nowPlaying screen tap anywhere on the progress bar to skip ahead or behind in the song. 

- Drag the progress bar ball to track the song to where you would like to play it from.

- Return to the list of songs by clicking the chevron in the top left.
